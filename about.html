About Us

Local Carpet Cleaning Experts in Austin, TX

FreshNest Carpet Care was founded to raise the standard for carpet cleaning in Austin with transparent service, premium equipment, and eco-responsible products.

Our Mission

We help families and businesses maintain cleaner, healthier spaces through dependable carpet and upholstery care. Every project is handled by trained professionals who respect your property and your time.

What Makes Us Different

  • Licensed, bonded, and insured for residential and commercial jobs
  • Truck-mounted and portable systems for all property types
  • Eco-friendly solutions safe for kids and pets
  • Detailed pre-inspection and post-cleaning walkthrough
  • Clear communication, on-time arrival, and honest pricing

Our Process

1. Inspection

We assess carpet fibers, high-traffic lanes, and stain conditions to choose the right treatment plan.

2. Deep Cleaning

Pre-treatment, agitation, and steam extraction lift embedded soil and restore appearance.

3. Final Grooming

We groom fibers, apply optional protector, and share maintenance tips for longer-lasting results.
