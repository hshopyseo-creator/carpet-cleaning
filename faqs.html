FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Carpet Cleaning in Austin, TX

How long does carpet take to dry after cleaning?

Most carpets dry in 4 to 8 hours depending on ventilation, humidity, and carpet thickness.

Are your products safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic products designed for family and pet-safe homes.

Do you move furniture?

We move light furniture pieces and clean around heavy items unless otherwise requested.

Can you remove all stains?

Many stains can be fully removed, but outcomes depend on stain type, age, and prior treatment attempts.

Do you offer same-day service in Austin?

Same-day service may be available based on schedule. Call us for current openings.

How often should I clean my carpets?

We recommend every 6-12 months for homes and every 3-6 months for high-traffic commercial spaces.
